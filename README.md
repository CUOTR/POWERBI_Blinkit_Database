# EXCEL-POWERBI-Blinkit-Database
# Overview
This file contains a multi-page business intelligence dashboard for Blinkit. It is structured into four main analytical views: Intro, Marketing, Customer & Feedback, and Order & Product.

Source: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/yashmotiani/blinkit-marketing-and-customer-powerbi-dashbord

  **Insights:**
- The marketing cycle slows down at the beginning and end of the year, while activity increases sharply in the middle of the year.
- E-commerce is the main channel driving business growth.
- The customer group that generates the most profit is new customers.
- The company's flagship campaign is the referral program, which generates the highest profit and has the best CPA, CTR, and CPM metrics in the entire campaign.

**Insights:**
- The core products are in the food and pharmaceutical categories, with the highest profit margins.
- Credit cards are the preferred payment method.
- Delivery times are longer during the winter months (a crucial sales season with year-end holidays).
